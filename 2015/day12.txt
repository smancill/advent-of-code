Setup:

    >>> from day12 import part1, part2

Part 1:

    >>> part1('[1, 2, 3]')
    6
    >>> part1('{"a":2,"b":4}')
    6
    >>> part1('[[[3]]]')
    3
    >>> part1('{"a":{"b":4},"c":-1}')
    3
    >>> part1('[]')
    0
    >>> part1('{}')
    0

Part 2:

    >>> part2('[1, 2, 3]')
    6
    >>> part2('[1,{"c":"red","b":2},3]')
    4
    >>> part2('{"d":"red","e":[1,2,3,4],"f":5}')
    0
    >>> part2('[1,"red",5]')
    6
